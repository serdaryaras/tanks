Tank Capacity Setup

Storage tank capacity = voyage fuel need + reserve. Service tank capacity = selected autonomy hours per fuel type.

Machinery Inputs

Equipment Type Fuel Type Quantity Power (kW) Load (%) SFOC (g/kWh) Density (kg/m³) Action

Formula per row: kg/h = Quantity × Power × (Load / 100) × (SFOC / 1000) and m³/h = kg/h ÷ density

Waste Water and FW Tank Setup

BV rule-based daily generation is always used. Total grey water is calculated as Grey(excl. laundry+galley) + Laundry + Galley. Grey and Black tank capacities are based on no-discharge period. FW tank is based on full voyage autonomy/endurance and total daily grey+black generation.

Sludge Tank Capacity (MARPOL Annex I Reg. 12.1)

Main discriminator: for ships on/after 31 Dec 1990, guidance indicates using items .4/.5 in lieu of .1/.2.

Bilge Water Holding & Drain Oil Tank (MEPC/Circular.235)

Main engine rating P is taken automatically from Machinery Inputs (sum of Main Engine quantity x power kW). Drain/leakage oil tank uses P and D based method from MEPC/Circular.235.
